How many font are supported to convert?
We support 3 fonts - Unicode,Zawgyi,WinInnwa.

Can this app usable on Mac?
Yes,it can be used on any OS and any browser.

How to use this app?
It's very simple with just 3 steps.First,choose your source font.Second,paste your selected texts.Then choose your desire font and click the convert button.

Convert button is not working:pressing the button show no changes?
It can be due to connection problems.Please check your internet connection and refresh the page.

Converted result is wrong and cannot be readable,how to fix it?
It can be font embed problem or our convert algorithm problem.Please contact to support.

Do I need to pay for using this app?
No,it's totally free to use.
